About Us

Computer Society of India (CSI)

The largest body of computer professionals in India, Oct,2014

Computer Society of India (CSI) started on 6th March, 1965 by a few computer professionals and has now grown to be the national body representing computer professionals. Since its inception, CSI has been instrumental in guiding the Indian IT industry down the right path.

Computer Society of India regularly organizes workshops, seminars and technical talks for the benefits of students and IT professionals which ultimately help them in their careers.

The Society also encourages and assists professionals to maintain integrity and competence of the profession and fosters a sense of partnership amongst members. CSI has 72 chapters across India, 511 student branches, and more than 115,000 members including India’s most famous IT industry leaders, brilliant scientists and dedicated academicians. Some of benefits of CSI are:-

  • Appreciation Certificate for performance excellence and Industry recognized CSI certification opportunities.
  • Access to journals and CSI knowledge portal.
  • Interaction and collaboration with distinguished academicians, researchers and practitioners
  • Eligibility to be nominated for the best Student paper in CSI communications
  • Opportunity to interact with Industry professionals and chalk out a career path

ProjectExpo, MGIT

Presenting of Projects of Alumnus,2017

The objective of ProjectExpo is to make the students learn from their seniors on:

  • Factors influenced/considered in selecting a project
  • How Technical and infrastructure related challenges were resolved?
  • How did it help them in campus placements or interviews?
  • In what way the experience gained from this project helped them to get job?
  • Criticality of presentation skills matching to the worth of the project executed
  • How the team work and their interaction with faculty was strategized
The best among all the projects will be awarded attractive cash prize.
